What We Provide To You

Industry 4.0 practices

Industry 4.0 Technologies IoT in Manufacturing and Maintenance Predictive Maintenance Data Analysis in Real Time Performance Metrics Remote Asset Management.

Machine Operator Courses

A combination of hands-on working on equipment under guidance, classroom learning, field demonstrations and real-life operations experience, supported by the equipment or machine manufacturers for contents and operations. Involves demonstrated capability to independently work in field.

Best Practices

Supervisors and shop floor managers are trained with a combination of on-shop floor and classroom sessions, focusing on practices to be integrated in regular work. Involves executing small projects and assignments towards brining improvement in real working.

Accelerated Operator Training

Each machine, equipment, or plant manufacturer needs trained manpower to operate their products. Most of the operators learn by informal techniques which sometimes leads to higher equipment failure in field resulting into production losses and monetary setback to equipment owners.

Material Handling and Equipment Operations

Material Identification, Storage, Handling, and Transportation Material Handling Equipment Operations Specific Equipment Operations Function Specific Operations Data Collection Understanding Visual Controls Work Reporting Quality Aspects Basic Regulatory, Business and Operational Compliances And many more...

Shop Floor Workforce Training

On shop floor / workplace training sessions over multiple short burst sessions of 20-30 minutes with hands-on demonstrations, technical and functional skills required to perform the job. Continuous observations, assessments, and necessary interventions for reiterations.
